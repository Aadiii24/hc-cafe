📋 How It Works

  1. Enter your UPI ID above and save.
  2. Print the QR cards and place on each table.
  3. Customer scans QR → menu opens directly on their phone.
  4. They add items → click Place Order & Pay.
  5. A UPI payment screen opens — they pay via PhonePe / GPay / Paytm.
  6. Order appears on your Admin Panel instantly.
