H&C Café – Table QR Codes

Print and place these on your tables

🖥️ Open the Admin Dashboard to view incoming orders in real time Open Admin Panel →

📋 How to Use

  1. Print this page and cut out the QR cards.
  2. Place each QR card on the corresponding table (Table 1, 2, 3).
  3. Customers scan the QR code with their phone camera.
  4. They browse the menu, add items to cart, and place their order.
  5. On the Admin Panel, the order appears instantly with Table Number, items, and total.
  6. Mark orders as Served once delivered.
